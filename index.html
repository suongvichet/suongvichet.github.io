Research

Research Focus

My research focuses on flexible piezoelectric rubber composite materials for energy harvesting, dynamic sensing, and self-powered sensing applications.

The work includes material fabrication, dielectric characterization, piezoelectric coefficient measurement, hysteresis analysis, dynamic mechanical excitation, circuit evaluation, and integration with storage or sensing systems.
